F.A.Q.

How do I join the server?

Launch Minecraft: Java Edition, go to Multiplayer, click 'Add Server', and enter play.gearblock.net. Make sure you are on the latest stable version!

Is the server Bedrock compatible?

Currently, Gearblock is optimized for Java Edition. We are exploring Geyser integration for future updates to allow cross-play.

What is the "Backrooms Anomaly"?

It is a unique server event/lore location. Rumor has it that clipping through certain walls in the lobby might lead you to Level 0.

How do I apply for staff?

Check the #applications channel in our Discord server. We look for active players who follow the rules and help others.
