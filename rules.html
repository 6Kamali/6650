Server Rules

Keep the community gear turning smoothly. Failure to follow these leads to a ban.

1. Be Respectful

No harassment, hate speech, or toxicity. We are a chill community.

2. No Griefing

Do not destroy what isn't yours. Even unclaimed builds are protected by our spirit of fair play.

3. No Hacking/Cheating

X-Ray, fly hacks, and automation scripts are strictly prohibited.
