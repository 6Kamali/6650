Network Protocols

By connecting to play.gearblock.net, you agree to follow the standards listed below.

1.0 Gameplay

  • 1.1 Integrity Any use of hacked clients, X-Ray packs, or automated scripts is strictly prohibited and results in a hardware ban.
  • 1.2 Exploitation Intentionally using bugs or duplication glitches to gain an advantage will lead to a full inventory wipe.
  • 1.3 Combat Logging Disconnecting during active PvP to avoid losing items is punishable by temporary suspension.

2.0 Community

  • 2.1 Conduct Harassment or slurs toward players or staff is not tolerated and will result in a global mute.
  • 2.2 Information Security Doxxing or sharing personal real-life data of any player is an unappealable permanent ban.
  • 2.3 Advertising Promoting other Minecraft servers or commercial links in global chat is forbidden.

3.0 Survival

  • 3.1 Land Claims Players are responsible for using the golden shovel system to protect their builds on the SMP.
  • 3.2 Griefing Mass destruction of player builds, even those in unclaimed areas, is prohibited if intended as harassment.
  • 3.3 Economy Scamming other players during trades is forbidden; please use the official /trade system for safety.
NOTICE: STAFF DECISIONS ARE FINAL. TROLLING OR HARASSING PERSONNEL REGARDING BANS IS PROHIBITED.
