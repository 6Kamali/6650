Network Protocols

Failure to adhere to these rules results in disciplinary action up to hardware blacklisting.

1.0 Gameplay Integrity

  • 1.1 Client Modification: Use of Hacked Clients (Killaura, Fly, Auto-Click), X-Ray texture packs, or scripts is strictly forbidden.
  • 1.2 Glitch Exploiting: Using game-breaking bugs or duplicating items to gain an advantage will result in a permanent inventory wipe.
  • 1.3 Combat Logging: Intentionally disconnecting during PvP combat to avoid losing items is punishable by temporary ban.

2.0 Communication

  • 2.1 Toxicity: Harassment, slurs, or excessive toxicity toward players or staff will result in a global mute.
  • 2.2 Doxxing: Sharing personal real-life information about any player is an instant, unappealable permanent ban.
  • 2.3 Advertising: Linking other servers or commercial sites in global chat is prohibited.

3.0 Survival Specifics

  • 3.1 Land Claims: Unclaimed land is "at risk." Players are encouraged to use the golden shovel system to protect builds.
  • 3.2 Griefing: Mass destruction of player builds (even unclaimed) to intentionally ruin someone's experience is not permitted.
  • 3.3 Staff Trolling: The Staff Trolling system is used for community engagement; retaliating against staff during events is prohibited.

By playing on play.gearblock.net, you automatically agree to the protocols above.
