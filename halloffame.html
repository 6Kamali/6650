Network Timeline
2022: The Original Era

The shutdown of "Gearblock Survival," marking the end of the first chapter and the original community's first hiatus.

Nov 1, 2025: The Rebirth

Gearblock Reborn is officially established by 6Kmlx and Voids, sparking the drive to build the ultimate network ecosystem.

Dec 16, 2025: The 24/7 Shift

Server stabilized for 24/7 uptime on 1.21.1. Sky Islands and the brand-new PvP Arena (/warp pvp) were established.

Dec 19, 2025: The Portal Era

Massive overhaul: A fully redesigned Portals Room was built to house Eggwarz, BedWars, and the Resource World.

Jan 8, 2026: v0.6 "One Chunk"

Introduction of the One Chunk Challenge and Mob Hunt. Added seasonal events and major QoL improvements.

Jan 14, 2026: The Corruption Purge (v0.9.1)

A security breach by a hacker resulted in a temporary loss of access. The community held strong during the "Fuck off hackers" era.

Jan 20, 2026: v1.0 Milestone

Paintball, Whack-A-Mole, and Squid Game added. Survival reset to ensure a fair economy after correcting staff permission abuse.

Feb 17, 2026: v1.1 "Nuketown Warfare"

Nuketown map integrated with custom gun handling and the Ghost Sniper revamp. Firearms officially added to the arsenal.

Feb 24, 2026: v1.3 Wood Revamp

Spawn shifted to a cozy Wood-themed aesthetic. Note: Co-Founder Voids received a temporary "shame ban" for test-server chaos!

Mar 21, 2026: v1.5 THE AWAKENING

Current Era. Credit donation from Ethan Parker secured play.gearblock.net. Features the High-Definition Dynamic Tablist.
